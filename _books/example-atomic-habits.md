## Key ideas
- You don't rise to the level of your goals; you fall to the level of your systems. Systems over goals...
- **1% better every day** compounds into remarkable results over time.
- Focus on **identity, not outcomes**, "I am a runner" beats "I want to run."
- Every action you take is a vote for the type of person you wish to become. Actions are evidences. Make evidences for the identity you want to live. 
- Make good habits **obvious, attractive, easy, and satisfying**; invert the four
  laws to break bad ones.
- **Environment design** beats willpower. Shape the space, not the self.
