This is my corner of the internet — a place to think out loud about the things
I'm curious about: science, math, money, and whatever I'm learning in CS and AI.

## Why I'm writing

Writing forces clarity. If I can't explain an idea simply, I probably don't
understand it yet. So expect rough notes, half-formed ideas, and the occasional
thing I'm genuinely proud of.

## What to expect

- **Blog** — essays and notes as I learn.
- **Book summaries** — takeaways from what I read.
- **Portfolio** — things I've built.

> To write a new post, drop a markdown file in `_posts/` and push. That's it.

Thanks for reading.
